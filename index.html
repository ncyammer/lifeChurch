WHO WE ARE


Located in West Los Angeles, we want to share "Life in Christ, Life Together" with you, your family, and with our community.


Every Sunday, we invite you to join our worship service, with heartfelt praise and life-transforming messages, while the kids have a great time at JOYland (our childrenâ€™s ministry).

MEET OUR PASTOR


Abel Kimm

pastorabel@lifebaptist.org

Abel Kimm grew up in Los Angeles. He loves all that is LA. He's building LIFE Church to be a church that is good for the city of Greater LA and beyond. He received a Masters degree in Theology (M.Div) to seek the meaning of love, life and God. His 20+ years of ministry experience spans across various places, denominations, and races. He loves God, people and life. He strives to shepherd LIFE Church to learn, live, and love in the way of Jesus. With his spare time, he likes to talk to God while surfing, write songs, golf, and play tennis. As a father of 2 kids, he is learning to love sacrificially from his wife.

SUNDAY WORSHIP SERVICE


Our worship service starts at 11 am with a time of welcome and announcements followed by an opening prayer and praise. Then one of our pastors shares a message from the Bible. At the end of the service, anyone who wants to can come forward in response to the message to be prayed for by the pastors or staff. Sometimes we also have testimonies, scripture recitation, and skits. Our worship services are about 75-90 minutes. Please join us for lunch afterwards--we'd love to get to know you!
