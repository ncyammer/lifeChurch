JOYLand


JOYland, our children’s department, is about parents and teachers working together to lay down a Christ-centered foundation for the formation of Christ-centered identity in our children. JOYland is founded on the belief that true JOY comes from putting Jesus first, then Others, and then Yourself. Our vision is to raise children following the guidelines of the ABC’s: the Attitude, Behavior and Character of Christ. Our name comes from Matthew 22:37-39, where Jesus said the greatest commandment is, “Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind. And the second is like it: Love your neighbor as yourself.”
