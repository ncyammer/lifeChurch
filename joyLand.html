JOYLand Children's Ministry

What is JoyLand?


JOYland, our children’s department, is about parents and teachers working together to lay down a Christ-centered foundation for the formation of Christ-centered identity in our children.

What We Teach:

“People were bringing little children to Jesus for him to place his hands on them, but the disciples rebuked them. When Jesus saw this, he was indignant. He said to them, ‘Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these. Truly I tell you, anyone who will not receive the kingdom of God like a little child will never enter it.’” -Mark 10:13-16

We value young disciples and look to build a firm foundation for them as followers of Jesus Christ. JOYland is founded on the belief that true JOY comes from putting Jesus first, then Others, and then Yourself. Our vision is to raise children following the guidelines of the ABC’s: the Attitude, Behavior and Character of Christ. Our name comes from Matthew 22:37-39, where Jesus said the greatest commandment is, “Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind. And the second is like it: Love your neighbor as yourself.”
